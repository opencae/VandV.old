Case 6.3 for the 6th ERCOFTAC/IAHR/COST Workshop on Refined Flow Modelling

   Flow Around Surface-Mounted Cubical Obstacle
   Experiments by Martinuzzi et al. 

Available measurements
**********************

Mean velocity components in three directions (U, V, W) and five components of 
Reynolds stress (uu, vv, ww, uv, uw) are available at densely located positions
around the cube.

The data stored in the current directory are extracted from the original file
"test41.tar.gz" provided by the ERCOFTAC Database.

The first letter of the filename represents the content, "m" for the mean velocity 
components, "f" for Reynolds stresses, and the second to fourth letters represent
the individual variable name.

The fifth to the eighth character indicates the x- and z-position as listed below:

   fifth and sixth column:
   code  01  02  03   04   05   06   07   08   09   10
   x/H   -3  -1   0  0.5  1.0  1.5  2.0  2.5  4.0  8.0

   seventh and eighth column:
   code  01   02
   z/H    0  1.0

Examples: mu__0302.dat  :  U-component of the mean velocity at x/H=0.0, z/H=1.0
          fuu_0101.dat  :  u^2-component of Reynolds stress at x/H=-3.0, z/H=0.0
          fuv_0402.dat  :  uv-component of Reynolds stress at x/H=0.5, z/H=1.0


The data are arranged in two columns: 
- the first contains the varying coordinate y in mm
- the second contains the quantity to be plotted  (in m/s of m^2/s^2)
